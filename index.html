The Interruption

We often use the Past Continuous (long action) together with the Past Simple (short action) to show that one action interrupted another.

"Joey was looking at the waiter WHEN Sarah took his fries."

Stuffed vs Hungry

"I'm stuffed."

Meaning: I cannot eat one more bite. I am completely full.

"I'm hungry."

Meaning: My stomach is empty. I want to eat right now.

Dating Etiquette

Read the modern dating dilemmas below. Where do you stand on these restaurant rules? Discuss your opinion with your teacher.

1. The Bill

You are on a first date. The dinner was amazing, but now the waiter brings the expensive bill to the table.

What do you do?

  • A) The man always pays.
  • B) Split it 50/50.
  • C) The person who invited pays.

2. The Phone

Your date's phone rings. They look at it and start texting a friend for 5 minutes while you wait.

How do you react?

  • A) Say nothing. Be patient.
  • B) Use your phone too.
  • C) Say: "Is everything okay?"

3. The Taste

Your date's chocolate dessert looks amazing. You really want to try it, but they didn't offer you a bite.

What do you do?

  • A) Ask politely for a bite.
  • B) Just take a spoon (like Sarah).
  • C) Order your own dessert.
